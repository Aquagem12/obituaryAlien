Grey Alien Cunt

September 11, 2001 - November 23, 2025

Grey Alien Cunt, the young grey alien from the planet Mars. He migrated to our planet, roughly 20 years ago, to start a new life as his family moved here to start a fresh.

While growing up in Earth, specifically in the City of New York, he was heavily involved in the underground rap scene and there he meet the one and only FilthyFrank himself while Grey Alien Cunt was high out of his mind

Him and Frank did all sorts of things such as partying, taking drugs and alcohol, firing up mixtapes.

They had a tremendous influence of the underground rap scene in New York, influencing future rappers to take heavy inspiration of their works.

Sadly, Grey Alien Cunt was gunned down on the streets of The Bronx on November 23rd, 2025

He will truly be missed by family, friends and fellow rivals.
